Million Dollar Bacon

Description

Million Dollar Bacon is a decadent and flavorful dish often served as an appetizer or brunch item. It features thick-cut bacon that is glazed with a sweet and spicy sauce, typically made from brown sugar, maple syrup, black pepper, and a hint of cayenne or chili flakes. The result is a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and a touch of heat, making each bite irresistible. The crispy bacon, combined with the rich glaze, offers a luxurious and indulgent experience that's become a popular dish in many upscale restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound thick-cut bacon
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place oven rack in center of oven and preheat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Line a large rimmed baking pan with foil. Place a wire rack on top of foil.
  4. Lay bacon in a single layer on wire rack.
  5. In a small bowl combine brown sugar, crushed red pepper, black pepper, and cayenne pepper, if desired.
  6. Rub bacon with brown sugar mixture.
  7. Drizzle with maple syrup. Bake until caramelized and at desired doneness, 40 to 50 minutes.
